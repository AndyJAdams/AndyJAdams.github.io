Butterfly - Advanced

LEGEND

  • MC = Magic Circle
  • ch = chain
  • sc = single crochet
  • hdc = half double crochet
  • dc = double crochet
  • tr = triple crochet
  • inc = increase
  • dec = decrease
  • sl-st = slip stitch


LARGE WINGS (x2)

  • R1: 13ch
  • R2: 12sc in second chain from hook. ch1/turn (12)
  • R3: 5sc, 6hdc, hdc-inc. ch1/turn (13)
  • R4: dc-inc, 3dc, 4hdc, 5sc. ch1/turn (14)
  • R5: 5sc, 3hdc, 5dc, dc-inc. ch1/turn (15)
  • R6: [dc, dc-inc] x3, 4hdc, 4sc. ch1/turn (17)
  • R7: skip 1 st, 16 sc, ch2/turn (16)
  • R8: 3dc into 1 st, 3dc-inc, 3dc, 4hdc, 4sc. ch1/turn (20)
  • R9: skip 1 st, 7sc, 4hdc, 4dc, 2dc-inc, 2tr-inc. ch3/turn (23)
  • R10: 3tr, 7dc, 5hdc, 7sc
  • work 7sc along side, sl-st to join. Trim off
  • CHANGE COLOR AND TRIM IF DESIRED

SMALL WINGS (x2)

  • R1: 12ch
  • R2: 11ch in second chain from hook. ch1/turn
  • R3: skip 1 stitch, 9sc, sc-inc, ch1/turn
  • R4: skip 1 stitch, 10sc, ch1/turn
  • R5: skip 1 stitch, 9sc, ch1/turn
  • R6: 8sc, ch1/turn
  • R7: skip 1, 7sc, ch1/turn
  • R8: 6sc, ch1/turn
  • R9: skip 1, 5sc ch1/turn
  • R10: skip 1, 3sc, ch1/turn
  • R11: skip 1, 2sc, ch1/turn
  • R12: skip 1, 10sc along sloped edge, ch1/turn
  • R13: skip 1, 9sc, ch1/turn
  • R14: skip 1, 8sc, ch1/turn
  • R15: skip 1, 6sc, ch1
  • R16: skip 1, 8sc along outside edge, sl-st to join
  • CHANGE COLOR AND TRIM IF DESIRED

BODY

  • R1: MC - 7sc
  • R2-R3: 7sc
  • R4: sc-dec, 5sc
  • R5-R8: 6sc
  • R9: sc-dec,sc
  • R10: close and tie off.

Antenna

  • Wrap floral wire in yarn, and glue shut
  • I don't like using glue in my projects so I do this instead:
  • ---
  • ch 8
  • hdc-inc into last chain
  • 7sc, tie off.
  • *insert floral wire into antenna down into body segment

FINISHING

  • Attach wings and antenna to body where it seems appropriate to you.
  • Ideally in a symmetrical location, but it's your butterfly. Do what you want.



Butterfly - Simple

  • R1: MC- 1ch,[3hdc,3ch] x4
  • R2: *Into ch3 hole from R1
  • 1ch, hdc, 2dc, 2tr, 3ch, sl-st into top of last tr, 2tr, 2dc, hdc...
  • ... sl-st into center hdc from R1
  • into hole from R1: 2hdc, 2dc, 2hdc, sl-st into center hdc from R1
  • into NEXT hole from R1: 2hdc, 2dc, 2hdc, sl-st into center hdc from R1
  • 1ch, hdc, 2dc, 2tr, 3ch, sl-st into top of last tr, 2tr, 2dc, hdc...
  • ... sl-st into center hdc from R1
  • Cut yarn. Stitch ends to top of butterfly and knot.
  • trim excess to desired antenna length. DONE!
