ROSE

LEGEND

  • MC = Magic Circle
  • ch = chain
  • sc = single crochet
  • hdc = half double crochet
  • dc = double crochet
  • tr = triple crochet
  • inc = increase
  • dec = decrease
  • sl-st = slip stitch


FLOWER

  • 4ch, dc into first ch
  • ch3, dc into base of ch3 [Repeat 9 times]
  • turn work
  • 2ch, 7dc into hole between dc and chains [Repeat 8 times]
  • *You should have 2 open loops left*

LEAFS (change colors)

  • INTO FIRST OF TWO REMAINING LOOPS
  • sl-st,2ch
  • 3tr into petal hole
  • 3ch, sl-st into ch base
  • 3tr into petal hole
  • 2ch, sl-st into petal hole
  • REPEAT FOR LAST REMAINING LOOP

FINISHING

Roll petals together, sew from bottom to close off and trim. DONE!
