Amigurumi Lizard

LEGEND

  • MC = Magic Circle
  • ch = chain
  • sc = single crochet
  • hdc = half double crochet
  • dc = double crochet
  • tr = triple crochet
  • inc = increase
  • dec = decrease
  • sl-st = slip stitch


BODY

  • R1: MC- 5sc (5)
  • R2: inc x5 (10)
  • R3: sc,inc x5 (15)
  • R4: sc x15 (15)
  • R5: sc, inc [x3], sc, sc, inc [x3] (21)
  • R6-R7: sc x21 (21)
  • R8: sc, dec [x5], 6sc (16)
  • INSERT EYES AND STUFF HEAD
  • R9: sc, dec [x3], 7sc (13)
  • R10: dec [x3], 7sc (10)
  • R11-R12: 10sc (10)
  • R13: sc, inc [x5] (15)
  • R14: sc, inc, sc, sc, inc, 10sc (17)
  • R15: inc, [2sc, inc ]x5, sc (22)
  • R16-R19: 22sc (22)
  • ADD MORE STUFFING
  • R20: dec, [sc, sc, dec] x5, sc (17)
  • R21: sc, 5 dec, sc, sc (12)
  • R22-R24: 12 sc (12)
  • ADD MORE STUFFING
  • R25: sc, sc, 3dec (9)
  • R26-R27: 9sc (9)
  • stuff tail and close off

LEGS (x4)

  • R1: 3ch (3)
  • R2: 3dc (3)
  • R3: [3ch,sl-st] x3
  • 3ch, and sl-st back to original chain.
  • stitch on to body at R11/R12 (FRONT) and R16/R17 (BACK)
